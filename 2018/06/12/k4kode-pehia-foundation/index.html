K4Kode - A Pehia Foundation initiative

12 Jun 2018

K4Kode is an initiative under PEHIA FOUNDATION focusing to provide access to Information Technology for children with a goal to improve the participation of females and underprivileged minorities in tech industry.

Why Computers?

We are living in an age of information technology where every aspect of our life has its influenced by it, starting from internet of things to automation by Artificial Intelligence. Everyday start with an alarm set in our mobile phones and end by checking the notifications of your favourite social media apps.

Information technology is indeed our present and future, but how to crack into that field? To use a computer, you dont need to learn anything specific. Academic projects, assignments, video games,make your child close enough with the computers. But the main part is understanding how a computer works,which makes the difference. Programming is the linguistic management of computers and learning the language enlighten your child to bright future.

what do we do?

We have to,
